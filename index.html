01: Values, Motivations, and Goals

"Do or do not. There is no try." - Yoda

Goals are attainable if you set the right type of goals. Being clear and specific about goals helps stay on track and is a way to hold myself accountable. I have realized that setting vague goals can be a behavior that stems from low self-esteem and a fear of failure. Poorly structured goals can also serve as a way to avoid responsibility when I do not achieve what I intended. SMART goals encourage realistic objectives that promote measurable progression.
